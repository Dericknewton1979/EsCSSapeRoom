WHAT IS GOING ON!

In and attempt for some rest and relaxation, you had planned a vacation alone to the island Fernando de Noronha, Brazil. After a long obnoxious week of anticipation and a treacherous rat race through the busy airport you were finally aboard the plane and in the air. Now for a nice nap to kill some time on the 12hr flight to paradise.....

In the midst of a dream involving an isolated beach, a cocktail and your favorite novel you are awakened to the sounds of screams and violent shaking. It appears something is terribly wrong and the plane is on its way to the earth, and not in the way its supposed to be when all of the sudden everything is black, still and silence.....

You wake to the smell of fuel and the blurred sight of burning wreckage. As you fight through the pain in your head mixed with the bright sun to regain your vision your realize that it appears you are the only one that has survived this wreck!
